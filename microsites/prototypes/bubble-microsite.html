Bubble AI — No-Code App Builder

Traditional no-code with AI assistance. More control, visual builder, powerful for complex apps.

Overview

Bubble is the incumbent no-code platform. It offers a visual editor for building complex web apps with databases, auth, and workflows. AI features help generate pages and logic.

Key Features

Visual drag-and-drop builder
Powerful database & workflows
Native Stripe, API connectors
Strong for complex SaaS & marketplaces

Verdict (Prototype)

Excellent for teams that need full control and are willing to learn a steeper curve. Slower "vibe" iteration compared to pure AI builders like Lovable or Bolt, but more mature for production apps.
