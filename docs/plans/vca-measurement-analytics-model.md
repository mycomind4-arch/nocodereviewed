# VibeCode Authority Measurement And Analytics Model

## Principle

Growth is useful only if authority, evidence quality, and trust improve with it. Analytics should track traffic, conversion, revenue, and evidence health together.

## Measurement Pillars

| Pillar | Metrics | Positive signal | Caution |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Authority growth | Qualified organic sessions, branded searches, report downloads, return visits | Readers reach methodology, reviews, comparisons, and reports from high-intent queries | Do not optimize for irrelevant AI-tool curiosity |
| Evidence coverage | Publish-ready reviews, fresh source rate, benchmark coverage, artifact coverage, QA pass rate | More pages become eligible for final verdicts and distribution | Traffic growth is not valid if evidence coverage stays thin |
| Reader decision quality | Comparison engagement, best-of click depth, matcher completion, methodology usage | Readers inspect proof before choosing a tool | High CTA clicks with low evidence engagement can indicate over-aggressive monetization |
| Revenue integrity | Affiliate clicks, report leads, consulting inquiries, sponsor interest with disclosure readiness | Revenue grows from trusted surfaces after disclosure and freshness checks | Revenue is not success if it correlates with weaker rankings discipline |
| Freshness and correction health | Stale source count, refresh turnaround, correction notes, verdict-change latency | The site responds quickly when tools change | Old high-performing pages should not remain live with stale claims |

## Analytics Events

| Event | Purpose | Guardrail |
| --- | --- | --- |
| review_opened | Measure tool-level demand and review discovery | Segment by evidence state and verdict state |
| comparison_opened | Measure high-intent buying research | Track whether comparison has evidence parity |
| affiliate_cta_clicked | Measure commercial intent | Fire only after disclosure-ready state is visible |
| methodology_opened | Measure trust and proof engagement | Tie to previous review, comparison, or report path |
| report_download_requested | Measure authority product demand | Include report evidence window and sponsor state |
| correction_viewed | Measure transparency engagement | Connect to affected page and correction type |

## Measurement Rules

- Dashboards must show evidence coverage beside traffic and revenue.
- Affiliate CTA events must include disclosure readiness and source freshness state.
- Report metrics must include evidence window, sponsor state, and methodology view rate.
- SEO wins are not durable until affected pages pass freshness and QA gates.
- Every metric used for ranking or revenue decisions must be auditable by page, tool, and evidence state.

## Operating Outcome

This model prevents the site from optimizing itself into a low-trust affiliate property. VibeCode Authority should measure whether it is becoming more useful, more cited, more current, and more commercially viable without weakening its evidence standards.
