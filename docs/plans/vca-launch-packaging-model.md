# VibeCode Authority Launch Packaging Model

## Principle

Launch in controlled releases. Do not present final authority claims until evidence gates are satisfied.

## Release Packages

| Package | Status | Includes | Gate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Private alpha | Build now | 28-platform review directory, evidence capture admin, readiness dashboard, methodology | Internal navigation, local storage, and verification pass |
| Public beta | Next | Core 7 review dossiers, 3 best-of guides, 2 comparisons, benchmark proof, disclosure policy | Every public recommendation has benchmark, fresh source snapshot, artifact, review record, and QA |
| Authority launch | Queued | Platform Index, Prompt-to-App Benchmark Report, source watcher cadence, SEO templates | Reports use dated evidence windows and ranked claims pass quality gateway |
| Revenue launch | After trust | Affiliate links, paid report offer, tool submissions, consulting lead path | Disclosure readiness, monetization firewall, and fresh pricing evidence |

## First Public Release Bundle

1. Homepage authority layer
2. Reviews for Lovable, Bolt.new, Replit Agent, v0, Cursor, Base44, Windsurf
3. Best tools for founders
4. Lovable vs Bolt.new
5. Scoring methodology
6. Readiness and disclosure policy

## Launch Blockers

- No benchmark evidence for a recommended core platform.
- No source snapshot for pricing or feature claims.
- Quality gateway has not passed for public recommendation pages.
- Affiliate CTA exists without disclosure readiness.
- Best-of page ranks tools without provisional labels for incomplete evidence.
- Report lacks dated evidence window or supporting records.

## Operating Rule

Public beta can launch with provisional pages. It cannot launch with unsupported final recommendations.
