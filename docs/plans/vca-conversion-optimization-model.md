# VibeCode Authority Conversion Optimization Model

## Principle

Conversion should grow from trust, not pressure. Every CTA, affiliate click, lead magnet, and paid offer must preserve evidence state, disclosure state, and source freshness.

## Core Experiments

| Experiment | Hypothesis | Metric | Guardrail |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Review Verdict CTA Placement | Readers click more qualified affiliate CTAs when evidence state and disclosure appear before the button | Disclosure-visible affiliate clicks and review-to-methodology engagement | Do not test CTA copy that implies final verdict when a review is provisional |
| Best-of Evidence Badges | Rankings with visible benchmark, freshness, artifact, and QA badges increase trust and downstream review opens | Rank card clicks, evidence badge hovers, and comparison opens | Badges must reflect actual records, not marketing labels |
| Report Lead Magnet | A concise report summary with evidence window converts serious buyers into email subscribers | Report capture rate, return visits, and newsletter confirmation rate | Do not hide methodology, sponsor state, or exclusions behind email capture |
| Founder Matcher Completion | Intent-based questions convert uncertain readers into better tool choices than generic rankings | Matcher completion, recommended review opens, and avoided category mismatches | Recommendations must state fit criteria and evidence limitations |

## Lead Magnets

| Offer | Audience | Trigger | Required Proof |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vibe Coding Tool Shortlist | Nontechnical founders | After best-of or matcher engagement | Category boundary, fresh pricing snapshots, and provisional labels |
| Production Readiness Checklist | Technical founders and agencies | After production-risk sections or AI IDE comparisons | Benchmark failure taxonomy, auth/data/security gates, and export notes |
| Monthly Benchmark Digest | Researchers, buyers, and operators | After reports, methodology, or readiness dashboard views | Evidence window, changed rankings, source freshness, and corrections |
| Stack Selection Audit | Teams with budget or urgent implementation need | After repeated comparison, report, or production-readiness sessions | Commercial disclosure and the same scoring model used publicly |

## CTA Rules

- Homepage CTAs route users by intent before asking for commercial action.
- Review CTAs require disclosure, freshness, artifacts, benchmark evidence, and QA state.
- Best-of CTAs must show gate status and provisional language when rankings are incomplete.
- Report CTAs must preserve evidence window, sponsor state, exclusions, and methodology access.
- Matcher CTAs must frame recommendations as current fit, not universal winners.

## Guardrails

- Every experiment must track evidence state, disclosure state, and source freshness beside conversion rate.
- CTA copy cannot exceed verdict strength or hide provisional review status.
- Affiliate clicks are valid only when disclosure was visible before the click.
- Lead magnets must preserve methodology access, not replace it with a gated proof path.
- Revenue experiments are paused when pricing, policy, or benchmark evidence becomes stale.

## Operating Rule

Optimize for qualified trust. A lower conversion rate with stronger evidence engagement is more valuable than a higher conversion rate created by weak claims.
